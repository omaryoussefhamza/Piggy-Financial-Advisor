Piggy is a personal finance tool built for young adults and students that lets users upload their bank or credit card statements (as PDFs), automatically extracts transactions, analyzes spending patterns, and generates clear financial insights. The app stores user accounts, statement history, and goals.

### **Features**

* Upload PDF statements and auto-extract transactions
* Spending analysis by category, income vs. expenses, and savings rate
* AI-powered financial insights and recommendations
* User login system with persistent JSON-based storage
* View past statements with totals and history tracking
